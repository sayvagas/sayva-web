What is SAYVA?

SAYVA is a mobile app that helps you find cheaper gas prices nearby or along your route. Prices are updated by the community: users submit photos of pump signs, billboards, or receipts, and the app reads the prices and updates the map automatically. You earn points for contributing gas price updates.

In short: See where gas is cheapest near you or on the way to your destination, and help keep prices up to date by snapping a photo at the pump.

Launch note: SAYVA is preparing for an Ontario launch, starting with an iOS-first mobile experience and Android planned.

How it works

  1. Sign up with your phone Create an account with your phone number so you can contribute gas price updates and track your activity.
  2. Earn points Submit photos of gas prices (pump, billboard, or receipt). The app automatically reads the station and prices from your photo, and you earn points for helping keep prices fresh.
  3. Find cheaper stops View gas prices on the map or search along a route to your destination. Stations are color-coded so you can quickly see which are cheapest (green) or pricier (orange/red).
  4. Stay updated Subscribe to stations you care about and get notified when prices drop.

Features

🗺️ Map & route search

Color-coded gas station markers by price level. Filter by fuel type and find cheaper gas nearby or along the drive to your destination.

📸 Photo submissions

Snap a photo of the pump, a price sign, or a receipt. The app reads the prices automatically and rewards your contribution with points.

📊 Points & balance

Earn points by contributing gas price updates. Track your balance as SAYVA tests contribution incentives and community programs.

🔔 Notifications

Subscribe to stations you care about and get alerts when prices drop, so you can time your fill-up.

👥 Referrals

Share SAYVA with friends and help build a stronger community of gas price contributors.

📱 Mobile-first

Designed for on-the-go use right at the pump, with an iOS-first launch and Android planned.
